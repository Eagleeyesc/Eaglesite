Faq

Questions and Answers.

You may have questions before you sign up and we hope we can answer the most common ones here. Don't hesitate to email if you have any futher enquiries.

Is my payment with you secure?

All subscription payments are handled by Stripe who are a well known and trusted payment processor. We accept all major card providers such as Visa, Mastercard, Amex etc. We are working on implementing Skrill & Paypal payments in the future.

How should I manage my bankroll?

We use a system based on a 1-3 unit scale; with 1 unit representing 1% of your bankroll. In some extraordinary cases, we might stake higher than this if there is enormous value, but this is rare.

What time will bets be posted?

Bets will be added 7 days a week, with the majority of volume coming between 12:00PM - 03:00AM GMT. We expect a higher volume of bets to come Tuesday-Friday but this will vary.

Am I able to get a refund?

You are unable to recieve a refund as you are added to the private group immediately upon payment. Your subscription runs for a month from the date you purchase, not the 1st of the month. This includes if the buyer has their accounts limited by bookmakers.

Is all of this legal?

All of this is completely legal. Tax/VAT is paid on all transactions by us.
